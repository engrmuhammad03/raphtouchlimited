Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our flooring, ceiling, and wall services.

How long does 3D epoxy flooring installation take?

The installation time for 3D epoxy flooring depends on the size of the area and complexity of the design. Typically, the process takes:

  • Small residential areas (2-3 days)
  • Medium commercial spaces (3-5 days)
  • Large industrial areas (1-2 weeks)

This includes surface preparation, base coating, 3D design application, and final sealing. We provide a detailed timeline during our consultation.

How durable are metallic epoxy floors?

Metallic epoxy floors are extremely durable and long-lasting. Key features include:

  • Chemical resistance to acids, alkalis, and solvents
  • High impact resistance for heavy foot traffic
  • Slip-resistant surface options available
  • UV resistance to prevent yellowing or fading
  • Can last 10-20 years with proper maintenance

Our metallic epoxy systems are designed for both residential and commercial applications, withstanding heavy use while maintaining their aesthetic appeal.

What maintenance is required for stretch ceilings?

Stretch ceilings require minimal maintenance compared to traditional ceilings:

  • Dust regularly with a soft cloth or duster
  • Clean with mild soap and water for stains
  • No painting or plastering needed
  • Mold and mildew resistant
  • Fire retardant properties

The non-porous surface prevents dust accumulation and makes cleaning easy. Unlike conventional ceilings, stretch ceilings don't crack or discolor over time.

Can stamp concrete be installed over existing concrete?

Yes, stamp concrete can often be applied over existing concrete surfaces if they're in good condition. The process involves:

  • Thorough cleaning and preparation of the existing surface
  • Applying a bonding agent to ensure adhesion
  • Adding a new concrete overlay (1-2 inches thick)
  • Stamping with desired patterns while wet
  • Applying color hardeners and sealers

We assess the condition of your existing concrete during our site inspection to determine if it's suitable for overlay installation.

What is the cost difference between epoxy and traditional flooring?

While epoxy flooring may have a higher initial cost than some traditional options, it offers significant long-term value:

  • Lower maintenance costs over time
  • Longer lifespan (10-20 years vs 5-10 for many traditional floors)
  • No grout lines or seams to clean or repair
  • Chemical and stain resistance reduces cleaning expenses
  • Can be installed directly over many existing surfaces

The exact cost depends on the size of the area, design complexity, and specific requirements. We provide detailed quotes after our consultation.

Do you offer warranties on your installations?

Yes, all our installations come with comprehensive warranties:

  • 3D Epoxy Flooring: 10-year warranty on materials and workmanship
  • Metallic Flooring: 8-year warranty with UV protection
  • Stretch Ceilings: 15-year warranty against sagging or discoloration
  • Stamp Concrete: 7-year warranty on pattern integrity and sealing

Our warranties cover defects in materials and workmanship. We use only premium materials from trusted suppliers and follow industry best practices.
